How It Works

A private and managed property transaction process

The platform acts as the mediator from enquiry to closure.

01

Seller submits property

Property details, documents, exact location, and price expectations are stored in the backend.

02

Admin verifies property

The team checks ownership details, availability, price expectation, and listing suitability.

03

Limited public listing

Only safe public details are shown on the website.

04

Buyer submits interest

The buyer enquiry is captured privately and reviewed by the platform team.

05

Visit and negotiation

The platform schedules visits, collects feedback, and coordinates negotiation.

06

Deal closure

Agreement, registration follow-up, and commission tracking are managed.

Buyer journey

  1. Search property or post requirement.
  2. Get verified by the platform team.
  3. Receive suitable property options.
  4. Attend platform-managed site visit.
  5. Negotiate through the platform.
  6. Proceed to documentation and closure.

Seller journey

  1. Submit property details privately.
  2. Property is verified by admin.
  3. Limited listing goes public.
  4. Verified buyers are matched.
  5. Site visits and offers are coordinated.
  6. Deal closure and commission are tracked.
